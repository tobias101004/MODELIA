================================================================================

Company Name: Cardenas Real Estate (Cardenas Inmobiliaria)
Tagline: "The benchmark in Gran Canaria real estate since 1980"

Cardenas Real Estate is the leading real estate agency in the south of Gran
Canaria, with the largest offer of houses and flats to buy and rent. Since 1980,

agency in Gran Canaria, with 3 offices and thousands of happy clients.

honesty, transparency and excellence." Their mission focuses on helping clients

ease and transparency."

6.1 SERVICES TO BUYERS
----------------------------------------------------------------------
Cardenas assists buyers in finding properties and accompanies and advises them
throughout the entire process, recognizing that buying property represents "one
of the biggest investments of your life." They offer an outstanding portfolio
of properties for sale in the best locations on Gran Canaria and are there to
help every step of the way.

6.2 SERVICES TO SELLERS
----------------------------------------------------------------------
The agency helps sellers achieve property sales "at the best possible price and
in the best possible timeframe" through experience, IT resources, real estate
expertise, and updated marketing strategies.

Seller services include:
- Professional property valuation with detailed inspection
- Home staging advice
- 3D tours, floor plans, videos, and 360-degree drone photography
- Listings on national and international portals
- Social media promotion
- Buyer negotiations
- Post-sale administration
- Customized marketing plans to reach the widest audience
- MLS partnerships (Boican - 33 agencies, Agora MLS Canarias - 13 agencies)

6.3 SERVICES TO LANDLORDS
----------------------------------------------------------------------
Comprehensive property management ensuring landlords' investments are
"safeguarded all year round" after finding suitable tenants. They offer a great
selection of long-term rental properties in the best locations on the island as
well as professional support for landlords.

6.4 LEGAL AND TAX SERVICES
----------------------------------------------------------------------
Cardenas is "the only real estate agency in the South of Gran Canaria with an
office managed by an expert in real estate law and taxation." Their dedicated
legal and tax office was established in 2012.

Legal and Tax Services include:
- Legal and fiscal assistance in property transfers (including private sales)
- Complete handling until land register registration
- Personalized cost and tax calculations
- Fiscal representation for non-residents
- Annual tax payment assistance
- Multi-language contracts
- Lease management
- Will and inheritance assistance (specialists in foreign inheritances)
- NIE/NIF applications
- Utility connection applications
- Public body procedures
- Encumbrance removal
- Town planning certificates
- Construction legalization services
- Maximum security in transactions
- Quality legal and tax advice

7. AREAS COVERED (SOUTH GRAN CANARIA)
================================================================================

ARGUINEGUIN
A charming traditional fishing port with 21st Century appeal. One of the most
popular locations on the south coast. Home to the main Cardenas office.

PUERTO RICO
The primary tourist hub in Mogan municipality, featuring picturesque volcanic
valleys running down to beautiful beaches.

PUERTO DE MOGAN
Known as "the Little Venice of Gran Canaria." An idyllic fishing village and
lovely marina offering peace and quiet by the sea.

MASPALOMAS
A thriving tourist area on the southernmost point of the island that combines
great facilities with beautiful beaches and the wonders of nature.

PLAYA DEL INGLES
One of the most popular places to live and holiday. It's all about having fun
and making the most of the sun, the beach and the nightlife.

SAN AGUSTIN
An ideal destination for families, with nearly 700m of gently sloping dark
volcanic sandy beach.

Municipalities covered: Mogan, San Bartolome de Tirajana
